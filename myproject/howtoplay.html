How to Play Dragon Tiger

Game Overview

Dragon Tiger is a fast-paced game where players predict whether "Dragon", "Tiger", or "Tie" will win.

Objective

Predict which side has the higher card value, or if both sides are equal (Tie).

Rules

  • Card Values: A = 1, K = 13, other cards follow their face values.
  • Dealing: Two cards are dealt per round, one for "Dragon" and one for "Tiger".
  • Winning:
    • Dragon > Tiger: Dragon wins.
    • Tiger > Dragon: Tiger wins.
    • Dragon = Tiger: Tie wins.

Payouts

Bet Option Odds
Dragon 1:1
Tiger 1:1
Tie 1:8

How to Place Bets

  1. Select "Dragon", "Tiger", or "Tie".
  2. Set your bet amount.
  3. Confirm and wait for the cards to be revealed.

Tips

  • Tie bets offer high payouts but are less likely to occur.
  • Observe trends using the historical record (Roadmap).
